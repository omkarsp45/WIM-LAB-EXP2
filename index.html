    • Indian Institue of Technology

    Established in 1958, IIT Bombay is one of India's premier engineering institutions, known for its world-class faculty, cutting-edge research, and vibrant campus life. Located in Powai, Mumbai, it offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various engineering and technology disciplines, consistently ranking among the top engineering colleges globally.

    IIT Bombay is renowned for its innovative research initiatives, entrepreneurial ecosystem, and strong industry connections. The institute boasts state-of-the-art laboratories, a vast library, and numerous student-led technical and cultural organizations. With a strong emphasis on interdisciplinary learning, IIT Bombay fosters a collaborative environment where students and faculty work on groundbreaking projects that address real-world challenges.
